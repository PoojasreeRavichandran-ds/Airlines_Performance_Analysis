#  Airlines Performance Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

The **Airlines Performance Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive dashboard developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze airline business performance and operational metrics. The dashboard provides valuable insights into revenue, profit, bookings, regional performance, flight status, and customer ratings through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters, enabling effective business analysis and decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall airline performance.
- Monitor revenue, profit, and booking metrics.
- Track monthly revenue trends.
- Compare performance across regions, states, and cities.
- Analyze flight status distribution.
- Evaluate customer ratings across categories.
- Enable interactive data exploration using filters.

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards for Revenue, Profit, and Total Bookings
- Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis
- Top 5 States by Profit
- Top 5 Cities by Revenue
- Flight Status Distribution
- Region-wise Profit Distribution
- Region-wise Revenue Analysis
- Ratings by Category and Sub-Category
- Interactive Slicers for Region, State, and Category

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Revenue:** ₹3,867,602
- **Total Profit:** ₹1,702,588
- **Total Bookings:** 763

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue remained consistent throughout the year, with the highest revenue recorded in **August**.
- **Pennsylvania** generated the highest profit among all states.
- The **East** region contributed the highest overall revenue.
- Completed bookings accounted for the largest share of the booking status distribution.
- Revenue varied significantly across cities, highlighting top-performing locations.
- Customer ratings across categories and sub-categories provide insights into service quality and performance.
